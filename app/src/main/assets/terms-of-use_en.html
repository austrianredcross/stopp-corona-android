  1. About the services
  2. With its free online/mobile services, the provider provides the user with
  • information about the COVID19 virus (“Content”);
  • services for the assessment of symptoms (Questionnaire, “Content”);
  • services for the documentation of human contacts ("Contact persons") during the time of the COVID19 epidemic ("Log-book") with the objectives being
  • the prompt notification of contact persons, as well as
  • reporting the disease to the controller for the purpose of pseudonymous information of the actively recorded intensive care contacts at risk of infection

in the event of a medical diagnosis of a COVID19 disease ("Communication tool"), or a positive self-assessment based on the questionnaire provided.

  1. The services are provided by the provider in the form of a content-as-a-service or soft-ware-as-a-service solution (SaaS). The provision of access to these services shall be considered the subject of the contract. The services are provided “as is” while the user shall not be entitled to certain properties or functionalities.
  2. The Content services enables the user to receive information about a possible COVID19 disease and also has the intention of raising awareness among users. The user is responsible for evaluating this information. The provider explicitly points out to the user that the services are not intended to replace consultation with a doctor. In case of any questions about the disease and therapy, the Provider strongly recommends contacting a doctor. The provider neither draws any conclusions from the use of the customer's content services nor provides any specific therapy recommendations.
  3. The “Contact log book” function enables the respective user, in agreement with the contact person, to document the ongoing human contacts on their end device. The mutual data exchange, on a pseudonymous basis, between the end devices only takes place if the user and the contact person have both installed the app (on a voluntary basis). The user can choose whether he/she wants to activate or deactivate the automatically running “digital handshake” in the background.
  4. The questionnaire enables the user to assess their risk of being infected with COVID19. For this purpose, the user is requested to enter the symptoms that he/she has experienced into the interactive questionnaire. Based on the result, the user is given recommendations for action, which include, among other things, the triggering of the "Communication tool". For the purpose of preventing misuse, the triggering requires the entry of the user's personal data.
  5. The user is solely responsible for the activation of the “Communication tool” and the associated triggering of the communication process to the contact persons, whilst the provider carries out the notification as a technical distributor. Only those contact persons with whom the user has been in contact in the past days are notified. The time span of the users being informed depends on the current epidemiological findings. If the " Communication tool " is triggered based on a recommendation of the questionnaire and the user is subsequently tested negative for COVID-19, the " All-clear " function enables the user to send an all-clear notification to his/her contacts.
