Purpose of the association

During its national and international activities, the Austrian Red Cross aims to prevent and alleviate human suffering anywhere and at any time. It strives to protect life and health and to ensure respect for human dignity. It promotes mutual understanding, friendship, cooperation and lasting peace among all peoples in accordance with the principles of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement.

(Details can be found at https://www.roteskreuz.at/impressum/)
